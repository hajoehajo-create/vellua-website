01. Juli 2025 • New Release

Chiquita

In a world that constantly demands our attention, where the hum of daily life often drowns out the quiet whispers of our souls, the search for true serenity becomes paramount. vellúa (Haval & Joe) invites you to discover your personal sanctuary with their exquisite new track, Chiquita.

This latest offering is a testament to the transformative power of ambient acoustic music, meticulously crafted to guide you toward a moment of profound introspection and finding peace. As pioneers of unique world fusion soundscapes, vellúa continues their mission to create 'Spaces of Peace' – sonic havens where calm flourishes amidst the chaos.

Chiquita unfolds as an elegant tapestry of sound, inviting listeners on a journey far beyond the ordinary, a soothing escape for the mind and spirit.

The Multicultural Journey

Music, at its purest, is a universal language. It speaks not in words, but in the harmonious resonance of shared human experience. With Chiquita, vellúa once again demonstrates their mastery in weaving together a sound that transcends geographical boundaries and cultural distinctions.

Haval and Joe's distinct blend of Middle-Eastern and European traditional influences creates a truly global sound. It’s an auditory expedition, where melodies reminiscent of ancient Anatolian pathways meet the tranquil echoes of European valleys.

For our diverse audience, from the vibrant streets of Istanbul to the bustling metropolises of New York, Berlin, and Paris, Chiquita offers a unique bridge. It’s a track that feels both deeply personal and universally resonant, a true embodiment of global acoustic music that fosters a sense of unity through its rich, layered textures. Each note is a brushstroke in a cinematic soundscape, inviting listeners to paint their own vivid internal landscapes.

Your Space of Peace

In the relentless rhythm of modern life, finding moments for oneself can feel like a luxury. But with Chiquita, vellúa offers you an accessible retreat – a precious space of peace, whenever and wherever you need it.

Close your eyes and let the intricate melodies guide you away from the day's demands. Whether you're seeking a quiet backdrop for reflection, a gentle companion for mindful meditation, or simply an escape from daily stress, Chiquita provides that much-needed solace.

Allow this exquisite piece of cinematic world fusion to be your personal sanctuary. It’s an invitation to pause, breathe, and reconnect with your inner self, far from the clamour of the outside world. Let the gentle instrumentation wash over you, calming the mind and rejuvenating the spirit. This is more than just a track; it's an experience designed to help you rediscover calm, promoting mindfulness and deep relaxation. Let vellúa's unique sound be your guide on this sonic journey towards inner harmony.
