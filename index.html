DISCOGRAPHY

Since 2018 we have released a lot of music. We started with our 10 Track Album "Portrait of souls". After the first release we tried to dive deeper in our sound world and collaborated with outstanding artists like Ciwan Haco, Denez Prigent and many more.

Haval & Joe

Philosophy

We build a bridge between silence and understanding, utilizing traditions from the Middle East, Eastern Europe, and Central Asia.

Vellúan Language

In our music, you will hear "Vellúan" — an invented language designed to transcend words and communicate directly with emotions.

Through blending acoustic instruments naturally and authentically, our journey has been an exploration of roots and spiritual resonance.
