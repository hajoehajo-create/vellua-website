Featured Artists

Over the years, vellúa has had the honor of collaborating with some of the most influential and soul-stirring musicians in the global folk and world music scene.

Denez Prigent

Breton folk singer-songwriter

A master of Breton music, Denez Prigent's hauntingly beautiful voice has reached global audiences. His music famously contributed to the atmosphere of Ridley Scott's cinematic masterpiece, *Black Hawk Down*.

Ciwan Haco

Kurdish singer-songwriter

A legendary figure in Kurdish music, Ciwan Haco is a composer and songwriter who has defined the sound of a generation. He is celebrated for his unique ability to fuse traditional Kurdish melodies with modern musical landscapes.

Tara Mamedova

Kurdish singer-songwriter

Known for her soul-stirring and evocative performances, Tara Mamedova is a significant voice in contemporary Kurdish music. Her work beautifully balances traditional roots with fresh, modern influences.
