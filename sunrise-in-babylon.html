04. Juni 2024 • New Release

Sunrise In Babylon

Welcome to the release of our newest single, 'Sunrise In Babylon'. As we continue on our multicultural acoustic journey, it is always a profound moment to share a new piece of music with our listeners worldwide—from Turkey to the United States, from Germany to France. We crafted this track knowing how deeply our audience values finding stillness amidst the chaos of everyday life.

Every morning offers a new beginning, a quiet transition from the dark into light. With 'Sunrise In Babylon', we sought to capture the essence of this transition, translating the raw, ancient energy of the rising sun into a modern space of peace. The title itself points to the cradle of civilization, a nod to the deep historical roots and the blending of Middle Eastern and Eastern European acoustic traditions that have always heavily influenced our sonic landscapes.

A Multicultural Sonic Journey

This piece emerged from a very intimate moment of acoustic improvisation. By weaving together the distinct resonance of traditional world-fusion instruments with subtle, cinematic ambient textures, we aim to build invisible bridges. The music serves as a dialogue between the old world and the new, reminding us that despite our constantly changing environments, the core of human emotion—and the desire for a tranquil mind—remains universally identical.

The gentle, rhythmic pulse mimicking a heartbeat acts as an anchor throughout the song, accompanied by the organic strumming that feels almost breath-like. We deliberately avoided anything overly tense or hurried. We want this peaceful instrumental to be something you can trust when the world around you is turning too loudly.

Your Space of Peace

We know how difficult it can be to detach. Whether you are winding down after a long, demanding day or purposefully seeking a moment of mindful reflection in the early morning, we crafted this track as a sanctuary for you.

In 'Sunrise In Babylon', there are no demands on your attention. There are no fast cuts or aggressive tempos. Just the slow, deliberate unfolding of sound. Put on your headphones, close your eyes, and allow the organic instrumentation to carry you far away from the daily rush. Let this be your quiet moment, your personal sunrise.
